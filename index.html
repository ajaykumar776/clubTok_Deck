The Nightlife Experience is Fragmented.

Both party-goers and venues face significant challenges.

For Users:

  • "Vibe" Guesswork: Relying on outdated reviews and photos.
  • Fragmented Journey: Juggling multiple apps for discovery, reviews, and booking.
  • Fear of Missing Out (FOMO): Hard to know where the real party is.

For Venues:

  • Ineffective Marketing: Static ads fail to capture live energy.
  • Reaching the Right Crowd: Difficulty attracting specific demographics.
  • Filling Off-Peak Nights: Struggling to create buzz during weekdays.

A Multi-Billion Dollar Market Awaits.

Booming Nightlife Economy

Launch Strategy: Hyper-Local Domination.

Our plan focuses on creating high density engagement in one key market before scaling.

Phase 1: Bengaluru Pilot

Launch exclusively in high-traffic areas like Koramangala & Indiranagar. Onboard the top 30 venues and partner with local nightlife influencers.

Phase 2: Metro Expansion

Replicate the successful Bengaluru model in other major cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Goa, leveraging our initial data and learnings.

Phase 3: National Scale

Achieve network effects by becoming the default platform for nightlife discovery and booking across all Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities in India.

Multiple Revenue Streams.

We will monetize through a balanced B2B and B2C model.

Ticket Convenience Fee

A small percentage fee on each event ticket sold through the platform.

Venue Subscriptions (SaaS)

A monthly fee for venues to access premium features like analytics, promotion tools, and "Live Vibe" dashboard.

Targeted Promotions

Allowing venues to "boost" their events or profiles to a targeted user demographic within the app.

Booking Commissions

A commission on high-value table bookings and bottle service reservations made via the app.
